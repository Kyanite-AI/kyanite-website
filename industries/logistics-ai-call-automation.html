What the logistics workflow covers

Tracking and ETA calls

Routine delivery-status questions are answered instantly so customers do not sit on hold for information that should be easy to access.

Rescheduling and redelivery

When timing changes are possible, Kyanite can gather the right details and route the request into the right operational path.

Damage or exception intake

Important issues are structured clearly and passed to the human team with context instead of arriving as vague, high-friction calls.

Why logistics teams use it

Dispatch operators lose momentum when routine status calls and genuine exceptions all hit the same queue. Kyanite separates the two, which protects response quality under pressure.

Less operator interruption

Better customer clarity

Customers hear immediate answers and clearer next steps instead of waiting through long hold times for routine updates.
