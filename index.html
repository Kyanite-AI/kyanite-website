Real Estate Agencies

Problem: Agents waste 3–4 hours daily answering the same questions about listings, scheduling viewings, and qualifying leads.

Your AI Receptionist Handles:

  • Property Matching: Automatically matches caller preferences with available listings
  • Viewing Scheduling: Books property showings directly in agent's calendar
  • Follow-Up Automation: Sends property details via SMS/email immediately after the call

Result: Agents focus on closing deals. Every lead is qualified before the first showing.

Medical Clinics & Dental

Problem: Receptionists are overwhelmed with appointment booking, cancellations, and FAQ—taking time away from in-person patients.

Your AI Receptionist Handles:
  • Patient Booking: "I need to see a doctor this week." Books available slots in your clinic system
  • FAQ Automation: Opening hours, parking info, preparation instructions, insurance accepted
  • Prescription Refills: Collects patient info and forwards to medical staff
  • Cancellation Management: Automatically fills cancelled slots from waitlist

Result: Receptionists focus on patients in the office. Zero missed appointments from phone queues.
